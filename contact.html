Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about ZenTrade.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

We typically respond to all support requests within 2 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST). For urgent issues, we have 24/7 live chat support available in your dashboard.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes! Phone support is available for Pro and Lifetime subscribers during business hours. You can schedule a call through your dashboard or contact us directly at the number provided in your account.

Can I get help with setting up my trading journal?

Absolutely! Our support team is happy to help you set up your journal, import existing data, or customize your dashboard. We also provide detailed documentation and video tutorials.

What if I have a feature request?

We love hearing from our users! You can submit feature requests through the contact form, and our product team reviews all suggestions. Many of our features have been built based on user feedback.

Is there a community forum or Discord?

Yes! We have an active Discord community where traders share strategies, ask questions, and help each other. You'll get access to the community when you sign up for any paid plan.
